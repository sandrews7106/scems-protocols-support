Sullivan County EMS Protocols

This page provides basic support information for the Sullivan County EMS Protocols iOS application. The app is designed for use by members of Sullivan County EMS and affiliated providers to quickly reference local clinical protocols.

Who this app is for

The Sullivan County EMS Protocols app is intended for licensed EMS personnel and other authorized clinicians. It is not a direct-to-consumer medical product and it is not a substitute for local medical control, education, or clinical judgement.

Getting help

If you have questions about protocol content, access, or technical issues:

  • Contact your agency supervisor, training officer, or Sullivan County EMS administration through your usual channels.
  • For issues with protocol updates or incorrect information, follow your agency’s standard procedure for reporting protocol errors.

For general feedback about the app (bugs, UI problems, or suggestions), please speak with your agency leadership so they can forward details to the project maintainer.

Emergency & medical advice

This application does not provide individualized medical advice to the public. If you have a medical emergency, call 911 or your local emergency number immediately.

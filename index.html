Support Information

Sullivan County EMS Protocols

This page provides support information for the Sullivan County EMS Protocols iOS application. The app is designed for use by members of Sullivan County EMS and affiliated providers to quickly reference local clinical protocols.

Contact Support

If you need help with the SCEMS Protocols app, please contact the developer:

  • Developer: XpressoMedic
  • Email: xpressomedic@gmail.com

Please do not include any protected health information (PHI) in support emails.

Who this app is for

The Sullivan County EMS Protocols app is intended for licensed EMS personnel and other authorized clinicians. It is not a direct-to-consumer medical product and it is not a substitute for local medical control, education, or clinical judgement.

Operational questions

For questions about how protocols are applied in practice, or about local clinical policies, please contact your agency supervisor, training officer, or Sullivan County EMS administration through your usual channels.

Emergency & medical advice

This application does not provide individualized medical advice to the public. If you have a medical emergency, call 911 or your local emergency number immediately.
